About Me

Navigating the Intersection of Code and Creativity: My Journey in Computer Science and Digital Media

As a computer science major and digital media minor at the UCF Burnett Honors College, my academic journey has been a dynamic exploration of the intersection between technology and creativity. Each step has been a building block, leading me to discover my passion for both software engineering and graphic design.

The turning point in my academic and professional life came during an unforgettable software engineering internship at Snap One. While the experience was undeniably amazing, I sensed there was something more waiting for me in the vast landscape of technology. It was during this time that I was fortunate enough to cross paths with my mentor, Ashley Chozas, whose guidance would become instrumental in shaping the trajectory of my career.

Ashley opened my eyes to the captivating world of UI/UX design, revealing a realm where code and creativity seamlessly converge. The fluidity with which she navigated between the technical intricacies of software engineering and the aesthetic nuances of design was inspiring. Recognizing the potential for synergy between these two domains, I made the pivotal decision to pursue a minor in digital media.

This decision has proven to be transformative. Through the digital media program, I have honed my skills in graphic design, gaining a profound appreciation for the importance of user-centered design principles. My coursework has exposed me to the nuances of visual communication, interaction design, and the psychology behind user experiences. It has been a revelation, blending the analytical mindset of a computer scientist with the artistic flair of a digital media enthusiast.

My experiences have shaped me into a professional who stands out at the intersection of technology and creativity. Proficient in both worlds, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving. My journey has taught me that the most innovative solutions arise when technology and creativity are not viewed as separate entities but as symbiotic forces that can elevate each other.

Looking ahead, my goal is to invite others to join me on my journey in full-stack programming. I envision a future where I can leverage my expertise in both computer science and digital media to create seamless, visually compelling applications. I am driven by the belief that by understanding both the code that powers our digital world and the design principles that make it accessible and engaging, we can build a more connected and user-friendly digital landscape.

As I embark on this exciting chapter, I invite you to join me in exploring the limitless possibilities where technology and creativity converge. Together, let's push the boundaries of innovation, creating solutions that not only function flawlessly but also captivate and inspire. The journey is bound to be challenging, but it is in the face of challenges that true innovation flourishes. I look forward to the challenges ahead and the growth that will undoubtedly accompany them.
